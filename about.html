About


At the age of 16 I started photography, when my father gave me an old camera. The last 10 years I bought professional Canon equipment and became a serious photographer. In 2019 I got a professional TCI qualification. Beside my job as a medical specialist in a general hospital, I can take photography orders. My interest lies primary in landscape, interior and travel photography. You can contact me here by e-mail or social media if you have an assignment or a special request.
